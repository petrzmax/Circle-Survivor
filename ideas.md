## Waves with Different Approach
- Few but strong enemies (e.g., shooting large laser through entire map length)
- Forces strategy change compared to many weak mobs

## Power-ups
- **Baits (Lures)** - place strategically
  - Automatically like a mine
  - Baits enemies untill they die
- **Slowdown** - slows mobs around player
- **Knockback** - pushes enemies away
